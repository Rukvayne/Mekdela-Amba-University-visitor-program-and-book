Mekdela Amba University

Mekdela Amba University is located in the historic area of Mekdela Amba near Gimba. This place is historically important in Ethiopian history because Emperor Tewodros II ended his life here while defending Ethiopia. The university is surrounded by beautiful mountains, historical sites, and cultural heritage. Visitors can explore:

  • Historic Mekdela mountain
  • The site connected to Emperor Tewodros II
  • The university campus
  • Beautiful landscapes

About Gimba City

Gimba is a peaceful town located near Mekdela Amba. The area is known for its historic heritage, beautiful mountains, and cultural traditions. Visitors who come here can experience:

  • Historic sites of Emperor Tewodros II
  • Local Ethiopian culture
  • Beautiful mountain views
  • Traditional foods

Visitors can book tours to explore both Mekdela Amba University and Gimba city attractions.
